A Little More About Me

Hi – I’m Brienne Sambell. I’m a recent graduate of Graphic Design at Fanshawe College and now currently enrolled in the Interactive Media Specialist program. What prompted me to go to school for a second time was so that I could focus more on coding and building those skills because I had always had an interest in going down that career path.

With the combination of my graphic design background and client side coding that I’ve learned in my current program, I see myself becoming a front end web developer. I enjoy coming up with solutions to a problem in a creative way and surpassing expectations. Working in this industry gives me inspiration, keeps me motivated to become better in my work, and I hope to continue to make more connections along the way.
