A Little More About Me

Hi – I’m Brienne Sambell. I’m a third year graphic design student attending Fanshawe College. What prompted me to go to school for it and what I love most about this industry is that it’s art mixed with technology, psychology, and business. I’ve always had an interest in photo editing and designing before I knew you could turn that into a career.

For the past three years I have learned so much and developed skills that you can’t get from a tutorial online. I enjoy coming up with solutions to a problem in a creative way and surpassing expectations. Designing gives me inspiration, keeps me motivated to become better in my work, and I hope to continue to make more connections.
